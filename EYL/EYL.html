About us

NO FIND - NO FEE - NO RISK

Eagle Eye Locators is a people search firm that specializes in locating missing persons such as debtors, witnesses, defendants, plaintiffs, adoptee's, beneficiaries on a worldwide basis. We pride ourselves in offering accurate and confirmed information with the best rates in North America.

Debtors

We can help you solve the problem of locating evasive debtors, helping to reduce the number of accounts that are written off because no address is known. Our skip tracing service is a fast and cost effective solution, which enables you to re-establish contact with your customers and collect your debt.

Plaintiffs / Defendants / Witnesses

These individuals are vital to your case and without them you may not be able to proceed. We can provide you an 'Address for Service' or a just a means to contact the individual if that's what you require.

Beneficiaries / Heirs

Whether they are 'Named Beneficiaries', 'Unknown Heirs' or you need to locate an individual for 'Notice Only', we have the resources to find these individuals, no matter how little information you have or how old the information is.

Adoptee / Birth Parents

Often these are the hardest type of searches as there is usually limited information available. We have excellent historical resources and have reunited many families.
